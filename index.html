What the line is doing

y = mx + b

This program draws one straight line and keeps adjusting it until it sits as close as possible to every point on the grid.

  • m is the slope — how steep the line is
  • b is the intercept — where the line starts, on the left edge

Click anywhere on the grid to add a point. Once you've added a few, the line will start moving to fit them.
